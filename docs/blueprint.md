# **App Name**: Ferdinan Strategy Curator

## Core Features:

- Headline Generator: AI-powered tool to generate compelling headlines for the landing page based on the sections 'Hero', 'The Pain and Thesis'. It takes the product descriptions as input and provides the suggestion for marketing contents.
- Content Refinement AI: Use AI to refine content in the 'Pain and Thesis', 'Curation Process', 'Validated Solutions', and 'Social Proof' sections, focusing on strategic messaging and exclusivity.
- Lead Pre-qualification Form: Implement a form with pre-qualification fields to filter high-value leads, improving the quality of consultation requests.
- Affiliate Integration Display: Design a minimalist card for displaying integrated affiliate solutions, featuring a validated seal and a refined title, avoiding third-party product imagery.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Rich gold (#D4AF37) to evoke sophistication and exclusivity.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC) to provide a soft, premium feel.
- Accent color: Deep brown (#8B4513) for a touch of earthiness and trust, used in CTAs.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif, brings elegance to the hero section.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' for a clean and accessible reading experience.
- Use minimalist gold icons to represent each pillar of the curation process, enhancing the premium feel.
- Cards: Minimalist cards featuring the product icon.
- Subtle loading animations during the form submission
